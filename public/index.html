Darjeeling (dahr-jee-ling) noun
  1. Commonly known as the ‘champagne’ of teas
  2. Straw in colour with a peach tint that also comes through on tasting
  3. Aroma likened to that of the muscatel grape
  4. Fruit notes make this wine an ideal accompaniment for coriander, cardamom and fish based dishes
  5. Chill to 9°C before drinking
