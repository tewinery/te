té (tei) noun An innovative wine maker that makes wine from tea

Wine (wain) noun At té we select the finest tea and extract its full range of flavours and aromatics and capture them inside a fine wine

Darjeeling (dahr-jee-ling) noun
  1. Commonly known as the ‘champagne’ of teas
  2. Straw with a peach tint that also comes through on tasting
  3. Aroma likened to that of the muscatel grape
  4. Gentle fruit notes make this wine an ideal accompaniment for coriander, cardamom and fish based dishes
