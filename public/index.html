té is an innovative wine maker that makes wine from tea

At té we select the finest teas then using a pioneering method extract the full range of aromatics and flavours and then capture them inside a fine wine

Our first release is Darjeeling which is commonly known as the champagne of teas. The wine is straw in colour with a peach tint that also comes through when tasting. Its aroma is likened to that of the muscatel grape and the gentle fruit notes make this wine an ideal accompaniment for coriander, cardamom and fish based dishes.
